# PRODUCT.md — Apexure MarkUp

register: product

## Product purpose
A visual feedback tool for agencies. Team members upload design mockups and
collect pinned, contextual comments from clients directly on the artwork —
click anywhere to drop a numbered pin, thread a discussion, resolve it. Replaces
scattered email/Slack feedback with one canonical, position-anchored source of
truth per design.

## Users
- **Uploader / agency member** (primary): creates projects, uploads mockups,
  triages and resolves feedback. Lives in this tool daily; values speed, density,
  and keyboard-fast triage.
- **Reviewer / client** (secondary): opens a shared design, leaves comments.
  Less technical, occasional, often on a laptop mid-meeting. Values obviousness
  and zero friction — must never feel lost.

## Brand
Apexure — a conversion-focused, data-driven web-design agency. Brand tone:
professional, precise, quietly confident. Minimal, not flashy. The interface
should feel like a well-run studio: calm, orderly, deliberate. The client's
mockup is the hero; the UI is the quiet frame around it.

## Tone
Direct, warm, competent. Microcopy is plain and human, never cute or jargon-y.
Empty states encourage the next action rather than apologize.

## Anti-references (what to avoid)
- Generic SaaS-cream landing-page look (this is a working tool, not a marketing site).
- Dark "developer tool" chrome — wrong for daytime client review.
- Neon/gradient/glassmorphism decoration. No hero-metric templates.
- Cloning Markup.io pixel-for-pixel. Take its clarity, not its skin.

## Strategic principles
1. **The artwork is the hero.** Chrome recedes; the mockup and its pins carry
   the color and attention.
2. **Two audiences, one surface.** Power-density for the agency, obviousness for
   the client. The primary action on any screen is unmistakable.
3. **State is always legible.** Active vs resolved, who said what, when, and
   where — never ambiguous.
